WHO ARE WE?

The Watering Can is a clothing brand in India that stocks chic apparel, home decorations, artisans, and accessories. It showcases a mix of Western Wear including dresses, capes, jackets, overalls and more. We work with the lovely artisans in Bengal, India. Collectively, we used our creative juices to bring together simple, usable yet aesthetically appealing pieces which can comfortably adorn spaces in your homes. We use cane, wicker, sabai, terracotta, wood and macrame to transform them into placemats, serve ware, and baskets. The Watering Can is here this time to narrate your story along with our own.
